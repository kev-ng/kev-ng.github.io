Move Ranger : A social and active game

Move Ranger is a Kinect game designed to motivate students to participate in physical activities. Using body movements as control, the goal is to eliminate the space invaders and survive.

Duration: 3 Months
Type: Master Course Work
Team Size: 1
Major Responsibilities: Research, Defining design direction, Ideation, Prototype, Testing
