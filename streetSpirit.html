Street Spirit: Speculative Installation

Street Spirit is a speculative installation that projects personal data into public space, highlighting the benefits and consequences of a future in which such information is widely available and mediates our experience of the urban environment. This project is featured in UQ News - Design exhibit has shocking way of revealing your ‘online footprint’.

Duration: 3 Months
Type: Master Course Work
Team Size: 4
Major Responsibilities: UXUI Design, Unity prototype, co-facilitate Design Activities
