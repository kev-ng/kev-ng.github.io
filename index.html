about me

Graduated with a Master of Interaction Design from Brisbane, Australia, I've designed web solutions and mobile applications for startups, SMEs, and Enterprises in my academic and work experiences.

I have had cross-domain training as a business analyst, UXUI designer, and developer. My design approach is to strive for a balance between business viabililty, human needs, and technical feasibility.
