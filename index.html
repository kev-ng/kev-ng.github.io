about me

Hi, I am Kevin. Recently graduated with a Master of Interaction Design from Brisbane, I am a UX designer based in Hong Kong. I've designed web solutions and mobile applications for businesses and start-ups in my academic and work experiences.

I have had cross-domain training as a business analyst, UI designer, and programmer. I strive for a balance between business requirements, human needs, and technical feasibility.

I am passionate about exploring new interfaces like VR and AR. In my free time, I actively participate in different hackathons as a designer/hacker. I embrace these face-paced experiments as they allow me to learn and solve complex problems with different people through design.
